Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to Zovada, developed by Built Fitout Developers. We believe in transparency and simplicity.

No data collection

Zovada does not collect personal data, usage data, device data, location data, or any other form of user information.

No data storage

We do not store user data on our servers, in cloud systems, or in any private database.

No data sharing

We do not sell, transfer, distribute, or share user data with third parties because we do not collect such data in the first place.

No profiling or tracking

Zovada does not create user profiles, analytics profiles, advertising profiles, or behavioral tracking records.

Children's privacy

Since the app does not collect or process any personal information, it does not maintain any database of information related to children or adults.

Policy updates

If this privacy approach changes in the future, this page will be updated clearly and publicly before any new data-related practices are introduced.
